Week 2

Morning

Homework review/Diagrams and shematics as a cognitive and aesthetic tool.

Afternoon

Project 1 - Creative Kluging

Homework

  • choose/find 3 examples of interactive installations that inspire you in connection with your creative technology interests and add links to your Github page.
  • [feel free to be broadminded in how you interpret "installation", so long as your choices are experiential and interactive. your examples might include electronic artworks, performances/stagings, commercial branded environments, theme park rides. or maybe they are installations which are not intended by their creators to be experientially significant, but you find them inspiring nonetheless.]
  • pick out one (or more) of the 3 examples, try to figure out how it was built, and create a diagram showing the components of the system and their relationships.
  • read Brett Scott, The Hacker Hacked, pick 3 quotes from the text and write 1 paragraph response for each (add to your GitHub repo).
  • be prepared to share your work and talk about your choices when we meet for next Friday's seminar.
